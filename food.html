Chinese Food

Cantonese dim sum

Guangdong has long been famous for its cuisine, and Cantonese morning tea is unshakable. A pot of tea with a few dim sum, three or five good friends sitting around a table to chat, has become a way of Cantonese social. Cantonese dim sum is very elaborate and varied. Here are some of my favorites.
Siu Mai (Steamed pork dumplings)

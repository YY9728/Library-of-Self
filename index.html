Guangdong Province, abbreviated as Yue, is the southern coastal province of the People's Republic of China. Taishan (the light red area in the image) is located in Guangdong's Jiangmen. The area is best known for its ocean islands, hot springs, and river drifting, as well as being one of the hometowns of overseas Chinese.
